**Modules (.mod .xm etc) are files that contain music data, similar to MIDI, except that it includes the sample data as well as note data, allowing the files to sound the same when played in different places. games and programs from the 90s until now have used this format to play music because a song that might be 10 megabytes as an audio file could occupy just a kilobyte of space. All of the XM files on this album are around 1 megabyte, whereas lossless WAV recordings of them tend to be around 20 megabytes.**
